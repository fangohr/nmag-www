nmag
====

nmag is a micromagnetic simulation package based on the general
purpose multi-physics library `nsim
<http://www.soton.ac.uk/~fangohr/nsim/index.html>`__. It is developed by
the group of Hans Fangohr and Thomas Fischbacher at the `University of
Southampton <http://www.soton.ac.uk>`__ and released under the `GNU GPL
<http://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl2.txt>`__.
